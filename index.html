About Tinycell

Tinycell is a mobile game development studio specializing in casual and puzzle games.

We aim to build efficient and effective workflows by actively leveraging AI-powered tools across our entire team.

Our focus is on delivering accessible, high-quality game experiences designed for a broad global audience.

  • Unity & Cocos-based development
  • Casual and puzzle genres
  • Global-ready production and release pipeline
