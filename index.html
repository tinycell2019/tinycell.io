About Tinycell

Tinycell is a mobile game studio specializing in casual and puzzle titles. We embrace AI tools across the team to work efficiently and deliver results faster. Our focus is to provide accessible, high-quality gameplay for a broad audience.

  • Built with Unity & Cocos
  • Casual & puzzle genres
  • Global launch-ready pipeline

Games

SLOT LIFE : Jackpot Journey

The world's first story-driven slot game!
Go from a broke to a legendary casino tycoon! Travel through hotels and casinos, chase your dreams of hitting it big, and grow your wealth and title through smart choices and strategy.

Match Cat : Triple Matching

Welcome to Matchy Cat!
Relax and enjoy a cute matching puzzle game filled with adorable cat-themed items. Match three of a kind, clear the board, and unlock new sets as you play!

Watermelon Maker : Fruit Merge

“Watermelon Maker” is an addictive merge puzzle game where you combine small fruits to grow bigger fruits and ultimately create sweet, juicy watermelons. With simple controls, anyone can enjoy the game, yet strategic merging adds depth and challenge. Cute graphics and vibrant colors make every play visually delightful, and the satisfying merging animation gives a real sense of achievement and fun.
